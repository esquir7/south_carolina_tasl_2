Things to Do in South Carolina

Mini Golf

South Carolina is renowned for its abundant and diverse mini golf courses, particularly in the Myrtle Beach area, often referred to as the "Mini Golf Capital of the World." Visitors can enjoy a variety of themed courses, including pirate adventures, jungle escapades, and ancient ruins. These courses offer family-friendly fun with imaginative designs, challenging obstacles, and beautiful landscapes.

Aquarium

Ripley's Aquarium in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, is a popular family-friendly attraction featuring a wide variety of marine life exhibits. Highlights include the Dangerous Reef, a 340-foot-long glidepath through an underwater tunnel, where visitors can see sharks, sea turtles, and stingrays up close.

SkyWheel

The SkyWheel in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, is a prominent 200-foot-tall Ferris wheel offering breathtaking panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean and the surrounding area. Opened in 2011, it features 42 climate-controlled gondolas, providing a comfortable and scenic ride experience for visitors.
